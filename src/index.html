About Me

Hello, my name is Hunter Parks. I am a Software Engineer with PKWARE. Learning is a strong passion of mine and therefore I am always looking for new articles and books to read and new technologies to become familiar with. When I complete my MBA in the spring of 2020, I plan to focus more on blogging and technical writing.
